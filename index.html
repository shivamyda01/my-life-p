Tanessha

Main tumhe sirf pasand nahi karta,
main tumhe **apni zindagi** ke roop me dekhta hoon.

Hum Yadav hain — yahan pyaar sirf bolne ki cheez nahi hota,
**zimmedaari hota hai.**

Aur humare yahan,
ek hi zindagi nahi… **7 janam** maana jaata hai.

Maine tumhe har nazar se dekha hai,
aur har baar ek hi baat samjhi —

**har janam ke liye,
ek hi princess hoti hai.**

Agar tum poochho,
“meri zindagi me kaun?” to jawab simple hai —

**Tum. Sirf tum.**

Main vaada nahi karta ki zindagi perfect hogi,
par itna zaroor — **tumhara saath imaandari se nibhaunga.**
